Jollibee Foods Corporation

Jollibee Foods Corporation, commonly known as Jollibee, is a multinational company headquartered in Pasig, Metro Manila, Philippines. JFC owns the fast food brand Jollibee and has acquired several competitors in the fast food business in the Philippines and abroad, including Chowking, Greenwich, Red Ribbon, and Mang Inasal, due to the success of its flagship brand. Founded in 1975 by Dr. Tony Tan Caktiong, Jollibee started as a small family business selling ice cream in the streets of Manila. Three years later, they converted the ice cream parlors into the Jollibee brand we know today. The family saw the opportunity for hot meals and put faith in hard work, passion, and focus to turn their small business into one of the biggest companies in the Philippines, Asia, and the world. Today, the Jollibee Group has 18 well-loved brands and over 6,900 stores in 33 countries, aiming to become one of the top five restaurant companies in the world.

Recently, the Jollibee Group acquired a 10% ownership in Botrista, a beverage technology leader, and owns a 90% participating interest in Titan Dining Partners II Ltd to further grow Asia Pacific food service brands and/or bring strong global food service brands to Asia Pacific.
